About Everask

What problem is Everask solving?

"Everask cares about the social distance exercise by ordering the masks from their own houses."

"Everask can reduce the danger of COVID-19 by delivering the masks to the users directly."

"No more "face to face" when buying masks."

"Everask additionally provides the map and navigation to the drugstores around the user."

"Everask gives the remaining numbers of the masks of each drugstores."



Our users

"Every one of you are our users. Connect Internet, get the masks from your place."



Overview of the Solution

"We have started to develop the idea of mask delivery service from the current COVID-19 crisis. By providing users to buy masks from their own places, we let users keep their social distance exercise even though they need more masks. As developers and owners of Everask, we have once experienced waiting in front of the drugstore to get masks. However, while waiting in a line, each of us has realized that the environment itself is critically dangerous since most of the buyers were not wearing the masks. From that thought, Everask was created. Everask will promise to support and to care for all citizens from the virus.
